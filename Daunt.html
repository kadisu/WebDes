Dauntless


Dauntless is a co-op action RPG by Phoenix Labs coming to PC in 2018. As it is still only in open beta it is guaranteed to become a beautiful and great game overall, with similar aspects of Monster Hunter games, hunting and taking down these massive monsters called "Behemoths" that draws their devastating powers from aether coming for the lands and destroying the people's homeland.
