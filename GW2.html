Guild Wars 2


Guild Wars 2 is a story-driven MMORPG developed by AraNet and published by NCSOFT. In the fantasy world of Tyria, players take the role of heroes dedicated to fight the Elder Dragons in order to maintain balance and peace. The game's world is persistent, there is always an event going on where a player can jump in the frail at any time. The game is grindy, but don't make it throw you off, with the game's "Living Story" which starts your character's journey to become someone big then on to the main stories that explains the problems of Tyria and how near it was to experience falling into chaos, the story itself is sufficient to take your mind off of the grind.
